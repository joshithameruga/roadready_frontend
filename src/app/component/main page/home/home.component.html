FAQ



Is there a speed limit?

RoadReady allows up to 125 km/hr. However it is 80 km/hr in a few cities where some cars might be equipped with speed governors as per government directives. Revv strictly advises to follow local speed limits.

Can I extend/ cancel/ modify?

Yes, extensions are possible subject to availability & charges. Cancellations & modifications will attract nominal charges as per our policy.

Booking criteria & documents?

Min. 21 years old, have valid original government ID (Licence only) and a valid driving licence for “Light Motor Vehicles”, which is min. 1 year old at the time of starting the trip.

Are there any restricted areas?

Leh/Ladhakh, Spiti Valley & Kaza/Nako regions are not permitted to take RoadReady cars. Customer will be fully liable for any damages incurred to the car in that region.
